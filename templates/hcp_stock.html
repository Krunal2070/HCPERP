Clear all entries
RECOMMENDED — preserves history
Soft-deletes every requirement entry for this product. The running total drops to 0. Individual entries remain visible in the Recycle Bin and the Requirement History view (with "show deleted" enabled), and can be restored later if needed.
⚠️
Override cached value
USE WITH CAUTION — breaks invariant
Sets the FG's cached requirement directly to the number you enter, without touching the dated history. The FG-grid display and the sum of dated entries may diverge afterwards. The next time you add or delete a dated entry, the cached value will be recomputed from history (wiping this override).
